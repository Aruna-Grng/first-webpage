Wonders Of Nepal

Nepal, a land-locked country between India and China, is blessed with natural beauty and its landscapes. It has a diverse geography, including fertile plains, subalpine forested hills, and eight of the world's ten tallest mountains, including Mount Everest, the highest point on Earth. It is a multiethnic country with Nepali as the official language. Kathmandu is the capital and the largest city of Nepal. Gautam Buddha, the founder of Buddhism, was born in Lumbini situated at the southern Nepal. This land-locked country is mostly known for the adventure lover within the country and internationally.
